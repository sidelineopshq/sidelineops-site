Why SidelineOps

When schedules change fast, communication often breaks down.

Coaches already have calendars, group threads, and email lists — but when a game is moved, canceled, or updated during a busy week, getting the right message to the right people can still take too many steps.

The Problem
  • Schedule changes are easy to update, but easy to forget to communicate.
  • Coaches often have to notify players, parents, boosters, and support roles separately.
  • Volunteers and game-day staff can get missed when updates happen quickly.
  • Programs end up relying on multiple apps, group texts, and manual follow-up.
The SidelineOps Solution
  • Update an event once and notify the right groups faster.
  • Work within your existing communication setup instead of replacing it.
  • Support game-day roles like concessions, gate, and other volunteers.
  • Reduce missed updates so coaches can focus more on coaching and less on coordination.
Not another messaging app. SidelineOps works alongside the tools your program already uses — like TeamSnap, GroupMe, and email — helping coaches send updates faster and keep everyone informed when schedules change.
