Not another group chat.
Program operations.

SidelineOps HQ sits beside the tools you already use — it replaces the manual work of keeping schedules, volunteers, and alerts in sync across GroupMe, email, and spreadsheets.

The short version: Keep GroupMe for team chatter. Use SidelineOps HQ for who does what, when — and let change alerts go out automatically when the schedule moves.
What you use today

GroupMe + spreadsheet

  • Free and familiar
  • Instant team chatter
  • Schedule changes need a separate manual message
  • Volunteer signup lives in a side thread or paper sheet
  • Parents who miss the thread miss the update
  • No public schedule or calendar feed
Program operations

SidelineOps HQ

  • Update schedule once — alerts go automatically

  • Public schedule + ICS — no parent login

  • Email-first + GroupMe integration
  • Coach actions in under 3 taps
