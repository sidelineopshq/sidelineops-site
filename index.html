Why SidelineOps

When schedules change fast, communication breaks down.

Coaches already have calendars, group threads, and email lists — but when a game is moved or canceled mid-week, getting the right message to the right people still takes too many steps.

The Problem
  • Schedule changes are easy to make, but easy to forget to communicate.
  • Coaches notify players, parents, volunteers, and support staff separately — using different tools.
  • Volunteer roles get missed when changes happen quickly.
  • Programs rely on multiple apps, group texts, and manual follow-up.
  • Meal coordinators, officials, and field managers are often left out of updates entirely.
The SidelineOps Solution
  • Update your schedule once — everyone who needs to know is notified automatically.
  • One platform for coaches, parents, volunteers, and support staff.
  • Volunteer slots, reminders, and help-needed alerts handled without manual follow-up.
  • Meal coordinators and officials get targeted notifications relevant to their role.
  • Weekly digests and cancellation alerts keep everyone in sync all season.
Core Features

Everything your program needs in one place.

SidelineOps is designed to reduce communication breakdowns, simplify volunteer coordination, and make schedule management effortless for multi-team programs.

📅

Schedule Management

Build your schedule event by event or upload a spreadsheet to create your entire season at once. Keep it private until you're ready to publish.

🔔

Automatic Notifications

Change alerts, new event notifications, cancellations, and weekly digests are sent automatically to parents and contacts via email and GroupMe.

🙋

Volunteer Coordination

Set up volunteer roles, create home game templates, assign standing volunteers, and let parents self-sign-up via a secure QR-code link.

👨‍👩‍👧

Parent Roster

Parents scan a QR code, find their player, and join the program in seconds. Contacts are automatically linked to the correct team.

🍽

Meal Coordination

Designate a meal coordinator who receives targeted notifications for meal time changes and cancellations — with player counts per team including call-ups.

👥

Multi-Team Support

Manage Varsity, JV, Freshman, and more under one program. Shared events, call-up tracking, and team-specific times all supported.

🔐

Role-Based Access

Invite coaches, volunteer admins, and meal coordinators with appropriate permissions. Each role sees only what's relevant to them.

📊

Schedule Import

Upload your schedule from a spreadsheet with full detail — times, locations, meal info, and team assignments. Duplicates are handled automatically.

📱

Public Schedule Page

A shareable, mobile-friendly schedule page for parents and fans — branded with your team colors and logo, no login required.
